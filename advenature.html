Zipline — 840m

Panoramic forest views.

₱500

Dahilayan Adventure Park boasts of two 840 meter ziplines with a drop of 100 meters. Zipriders are transported via 4WD Safari Cruiser to a launch tower at 4500′ ASL. Zoom through the rainforest canopy at 60-80kph to land at our Carrier-type flight deck using a special arresting gear system.

Weight: MIN. 40kg > MAX. 75kg

Height: Above 4ft

Dropzone

Adrenaline free-fall swing.

₱500–₱750

Single Rider: P500

Tandem/Pair: P750

Dahilayan features a 120 ft Freefall into a manmade lake. Swoop to within feet of the water surface making it an adrenaline pumping Screamer of a ride. This is the Philippine’s first extreme pendulum swing of its kind.

ADULT - P300 (All Levels)
